📚 Admissions at Madarsa Arabia Atharul Uloom

We welcome students who seek **quality education** rooted in **academic excellence** and **Islamic values**. Our curriculum blends **modern education with Islamic studies**, ensuring a well-rounded development for every child.

🌟 Why Choose Us?

  • ✔ Free Education – Quality learning without financial burden
  • ✔ Islamic & Modern Curriculum – Hifz, Alimah, Mubaligh, Deeniyat, and academic subjects
  • ✔ Nutritious Meals – Daily balanced diet for students
  • ✔ Hostel Facility – Safe and comfortable boarding for boys
  • ✔ Character Building – Instilling Islamic morals and ethics

📖 Courses We Offer

  • 📘 Hifz Course – Memorization of the Holy Quran
  • 📘 Mubaligh Course – Training in Islamic preaching
  • 📘 Deeniyat Education – Islamic knowledge and daily life teachings
  • 📘 Alimah Course – Advanced Islamic studies for girls
  • 📘 Academic Education – Age-appropriate learning with a structured curriculum

📌 Age Criteria for Admission

  • 🎒 Early Learning Program: 2.4 to 4 years
  • 📚 Primary & Secondary Education: 5 years & above
  • 📖 Islamic Studies & Hifz Program: Open for all eligible students

🏠 Hostel Facilities (For Boys Only)

  • 🛏️ Comfortable bedding arrangements
  • 🍽️ Daily nutritious meals (breakfast, lunch & dinner)
  • 🚿 Clean and well-maintained bathrooms

📝 How to Apply?

  1. 📌 Step 1: Fill out the admission form (available at our office or online)
  2. 📌 Step 2: Submit the required documents (Birth Certificate, Previous School Records, etc.)
  3. 📌 Step 3: Attend the admission interview (if applicable)
